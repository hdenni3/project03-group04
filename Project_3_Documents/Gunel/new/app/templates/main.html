YouTube Global Performances 2023

A Data-Driven Exploration of the Most Popular YouTube Channels

Introduction to the Project

As Data-driven Students, we intend to utilize global statistics of the most popular YouTubers. This platform showcases prominent international YouTubers and the corresponding statistics they generate, including their number of subscribers, video views, video categories, and earnings. The primary goal is to highlight the achievements of top performers and identify channels most likely to attract more views and subscriptions.

Purpose and Use of the Dataset

This dataset is specifically designed to assist YouTubers, researchers, and businesses in monitoring content creator performance on a global level. It provides insights into trends, user engagement, and potential growth opportunities, allowing for data-driven decision-making.

Features of the App

  • Interactive dashboard with customizable filters
  • Geographic analysis of YouTube statistics
  • Comparative analysis across different countries and categories
  • Insights into channel growth trends and user engagement
  • Data-driven predictions for future performance

User Guidance

Use the navigation links at the top to explore the dashboard, view geographic insights, or compare YouTube statistics across different countries. Customize your analysis with interactive filters and visualizations to gain deeper insights.
