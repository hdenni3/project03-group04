About Project 3 - Group 4

Introduction & Project Overview

The rationale for selecting this dataset is as follows:

As students who prioritize data-driven approaches, we used global statistics to gather information on the most popular YouTubers. The platform features well-known international YouTubers and provides detailed statistics on their subscribers, video views, video categories, and earnings. YouTube is a popular platform utilized by a wide range of individuals, not solely for gaming or entertainment purposes.

The main objective of the dataset is to showcase the accomplishments of high-achievers and determine the most effective channels for increasing viewership and subscriptions. This dataset is specifically designed for YouTubers to track the global performance of their content creators. It has not been modified for any other purposes. YouTube is used worldwide for various purposes, including education, research, and sharing business information.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to gain a deep understanding of various aspects of YouTube, including its impact on the world and its influence on people's lives. We strive to uncover the factors that contribute to success on YouTube and how the platform serves different individuals in diverse ways.

Ethical Considerations

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct throughout our project. We respect the privacy of all YouTubers and ensure that the data used in our analysis is publicly available and used responsibly.
